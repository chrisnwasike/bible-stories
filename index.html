About This Project

An interactive journey through the New King James Version of the Holy Bible — chapter by chapter, book by book. Each passage is paired with original artwork, interactive timelines, maps, and visual storytelling designed to illuminate the text for modern readers.

From the genealogies of Genesis to the visions of Revelation, every chapter receives a unique treatment crafted for its genre — narrative, poetry, law, prophecy, or epistle.
